Olive oil ice-cream

Description

a rich, sweet, creamy frozen food made from variously flavored cream and milk products churned or stirred to a smooth consistency during the freezing process and often containing gelatin, eggs, fruits, nuts, etc.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons water
  • 3/4 cup whole milk
  • Large pinch salt
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Slowly mix all the ingredients, except the eggs, together
  2. Beat the eggs up
  3. Add the eggs to the mixture and let it freeze for 12 hours
