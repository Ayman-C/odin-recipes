Lasagna

Description

This is Mary's classic Lasagna recipe which has been perfected over the years. For the best results leave the Lasagna to stand for 6 hours before cooking.

You can prepare this easy Lasagna ahead of time and save in the freezer, uncooked, for when you need it during a busy week. Then just bake for an extra 45 mins.

Ingredients

  • For the ragu
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 900g/2lb beef mince
    • 2 onions, roughly chopped
    • 4 sticks celery, diced (optional)
    • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
    • 2 level tbsp plain flour
    • 150ml/¼ pint beef stock
    • 1 tbsp redcurrant jelly (optional) or 1 tsp caster sugar
    • 3 tbsp tomato purée
    • 1 tbsp chopped thyme
    • 2 x 400g cans chopped tomatoes
  • For the white sauce
    • 50g/2oz butter
    • 50g/2oz plain flour
    • 750ml/1¼ pints hot milk
    • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
    • 50g/2oz Parmesan, finely grated
    • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • For the lasagna
    • 12 lasagna sheets
    • 75g/3oz mature cheddar, grated
